About EliteAuction

25 Years of Excellence in the Auction Industry

Our Story

For over a quarter century, EliteAuction has been at the forefront of the auction industry, connecting sellers with buyers through transparent and competitive bidding processes.

Founded in 2000, our journey began with traditional in-person auctions specializing in estate sales, fine art, and collectibles. As technology evolved, so did we, embracing digital innovations to enhance the auction experience for our growing community.

Today, EliteAuction proudly stands as a pioneer in the e-auction space, offering a seamless online platform while maintaining the personal touch and expertise that has defined our reputation for 25 years.

Our Journey

2000

EliteAuction founded as a traditional auction house specializing in estate sales and collectibles.

2008

Expanded services to include fine art, luxury goods, and rare collectibles auctions.

2015

Launched our first hybrid auction model, combining in-person and online bidding.

2020

Transitioned to a fully digital e-auction platform, revolutionizing access for global bidders.

Today

Operating as an industry leader with thousands of successful auctions and a global community of buyers and sellers.
